title_en: Model Failure Conditions
summary_en: The conclusion of the removal tests: the equilibrium's real load-bearing points are only two, the material layer and the structural layer; the other four mechanisms are amplifiers on different time scales.
overview_en: |
  Failure conditions: V1 stays below threshold long-term; the V2 breakthrough fails; catastrophe forces reconstruction under strong integration; a strong shared narrative raises V7; institutional innovation brings AI productivity back into labor-based distribution; overlapping failure of A2's control system; failure of A1's triple closure. Removal tests show that the material layer and the structural layer are the load-bearing mechanisms, while the experiential layer, K-12 formation, higher-education resolution, and Polarization Fatigue are amplifiers; the tests expose the Generational Handover Window (roughly 2065–2085) as the thinnest stretch on the equilibrium's subjective side — the 2072 steady state is a snapshot from the middle of that window.
