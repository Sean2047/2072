overview: |
  劳动是大多数人参与社会分配的主要机制；AI使标准化认知劳动的市场价值系统性下降，分配逻辑的物质基础被侵蚀。结果是真实的社区多元化：民主社会中社区作为主权主体参与共同管理国家，威权体制下社区是被允许存在的剩余空间。没有共同终点，只有路径的真正分叉：不是文明退步，而是同质化压力解除后历史文化惯性的重新浮现。
title_en: Fundamental Proposition
summary_en: The material basis of distribution according to labor contribution is being eroded — something without precedent in human history.
overview_en: |
  Labor is the main mechanism through which most people take part in social distribution; AI systematically lowers the market value of standardized cognitive labor, eroding the material basis of that distributive logic. The result is a real pluralization of communities: in democratic societies communities take part in co-governing the state as sovereign subjects, while under authoritarian systems communities are residual spaces permitted to exist. There is no common endpoint, only a true forking of paths: not civilizational regression, but the resurfacing of historical and cultural inertia once the pressure of homogenization is lifted.
