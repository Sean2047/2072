overview: |
  AI替代使边缘人口从"待整合的潜在劳动力"变成"不需要的剩余存在"，忽视比改造便宜；对抗的机会成本超过收益，相互忽视是双方都能接受的纳什均衡。三个支撑层：物质层（配给覆盖生存底线）、结构层（集体行动降维，选举通道可解域限于分配层）、经验层（冲突厌倦的集体记忆）。张力：忽视不对称、依赖配给持续运转、国家层面不适用。
title_en: Mutual Disregard Equilibrium
summary_en: A steady state of low conflict, low integration, and low expectations — the position to which rational actors on all sides naturally converge under the given cost structure.
overview_en: |
  AI displacement turns marginal populations from "potential labor awaiting integration" into "surplus existence no longer needed"; disregard is cheaper than transformation, the opportunity cost of confrontation exceeds its returns, and mutual disregard becomes a Nash equilibrium both sides can accept. Three supporting layers: material (provisioning covers the subsistence floor), structural (collective action reduced in dimension — the electoral channel's solvable domain confined to the distribution layer), and experiential (collective memory of conflict weariness). Tensions: the disregard is asymmetric, it depends on provisioning continuing to run, and it does not apply at the level of states.
