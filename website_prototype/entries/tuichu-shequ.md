title_en: Post-Employment Communities

overview_en: |
  Post-Employment Communities likewise need no confrontation: confrontation burns energy and requires organization and clear political aims, and once provisioning covers material subsistence and the expected returns to collective action run very low, disregard is cheaper than remaking. The tension between these communities and the AI-capital system is asymmetric — AI capital disregards because it has no need; the communities disregard because confrontation costs more than it returns — and that asymmetry accumulates beneath the equilibrium's surface.

**词典定义（v156 关键字词典，原文）**

> 从中心生产体系退出、但仍维持基本生存与地方生活的社区类型。并非彻底崩溃，只是不再被整合逻辑积极改造。
