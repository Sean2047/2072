title_en: Main Causal Chains (A–F)
summary_en: Six main causal chains: labor displacement, migration decline, stability without happiness, Mutual Disregard Equilibrium, polymorphic divergence, technology receding into the background.
overview_en: |
  Chain A, labor displacement: V1 rises → the value of labor falls → loss of a position of being needed → the Meaning Crisis spreads. Chain B, migration decline: returns to migration fall → local networks appreciate → reflux into community. Chain C, stability without happiness: survival anxiety falls while V6 does not strengthen in step → psychological adaptation is privatized. Chain D, Mutual Disregard Equilibrium: disregard costs less than transformation. Chain E, polymorphic divergence: the same shock × differences in V4 and V8 → the single future dissolves into coexisting plural forms. Chain F: technology recedes from foreground to background.
