title_en: Identity Vacuum

overview_en: |
  The industrial era supplied a clear identity frame — practitioner of a trade, member of a class, participant in an upward trajectory. That frame fails systematically in the AI era, and replacements take time to form. The Identity Vacuum heads the list of the real sources of turbulence: people who lose the language for who am I, where am I, why am I here are extraordinarily easy to activate politically, and this middle ground is the true hotbed of polarization, populism, and culture war.
