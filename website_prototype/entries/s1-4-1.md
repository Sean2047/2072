overview: |
  历次技术革命替代旧岗位同时创造新岗位，前提是人类仍被需要完成更复杂的部分。AI打破这个前提：不是新岗位数量不足，而是其认知门槛已超过大多数被替代者的可及范围。劳动收入占GDP比重系统性下降而资本收入高度集中，两个趋势叠加使以劳动贡献为依据的分配逻辑在物质层面失去基础——分配问题从政治争议变成结构现实。
title_en: Structural Weakening of the Value of Labor
summary_en: AI breaks through standardized cognitive ability, the "last line of defense"; the historical cycle of "displacement → upskilling → re-employment" loses its operating conditions.
overview_en: |
  Every previous technological revolution displaced old jobs while creating new ones, on the premise that humans were still needed for the more complex parts. AI breaks that premise: the problem is not that new jobs are too few, but that their cognitive threshold now exceeds what most of the displaced can reach. Labor's share of GDP declines systematically while capital income is highly concentrated; together the two trends strip the material basis from distribution according to labor contribution — the distribution question turns from political controversy into structural fact.
