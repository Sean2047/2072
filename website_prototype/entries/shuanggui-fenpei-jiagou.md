overview_en: |
  The system precisely maintains the visible material gap between having a work unit (the wage track) and having only vouchers (the voucher track); this is functional design rather than fiscal necessity, and the gap itself is the incentive core of employment binding. The difference in consumption autonomy between the tracks is one of A2's core stratification mechanisms: the waged population keeps its identity anchor, while the voucher population occupies the relative blind spot of official narrative.
