summary: 以综合实力为底层逻辑的七类角色分类；出口品类等阶段性特征不作为分类依据。
overview: |
  综合实力涵盖技术—经济能力、政治—组织能力与体量三个维度。A1（民主体系核心，美国）与A2（威权体系核心，中国）自身定义体系规则，性质不同于其余五类：B1民主体系整合者、B2威权体系整合者、C对冲自主体、D资源出口国、E边缘经济体。D类2072年内部分化显著；E类区分结构性脱嵌与冲突性脱嵌两种形态。
title_en: Classification of World Economic Roles (Reference Framework)
summary_en: A seven-category classification of roles grounded in comprehensive national capability; stage-specific traits such as export composition are not classificatory criteria.
overview_en: |
  Comprehensive capability spans three dimensions: technological-economic capacity, political-organizational capacity, and scale. A1 (the market-democratic core, the United States) and A2 (the state-coordinated core, China) define the rules of their respective systems and differ in kind from the other five categories: B1 Democratic-System Integrators, B2 Authoritarian-System Integrators, C Hedging States, D Resource Exporters, and E Marginal Economies. Category D shows marked internal divergence by 2072; category E divides into structural and conflict-driven forms of disembedding.
