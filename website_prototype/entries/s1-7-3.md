  精神健康挑战是多态稳定的背景变量而非威胁：特定人群指标有统计性上升，但整体社会痛苦指数随BSC覆盖和社区稳态形成而收敛。心理适应主要在个体和社区层面进行而非通过制度性渠道，这本身是多态稳定的内在机制之一。系统稳定与个体意义寻找并行，不相互取消——意义问题的答案仍在各路径、各社区、各个体之间持续被寻找和构建。
title_en: Mental Health and Polymorphic Stability
summary_en: Individual psychological challenges resist political organization because they are highly privatized — "I feel empty" cannot be turned into a political program.
overview_en: |
  Mental health challenges are a background variable of polymorphic stability, not a threat: indicators rise statistically in specific populations, but the overall index of social suffering converges as BSC coverage and community steady states take hold. Psychological adaptation proceeds mainly at the individual and community levels rather than through institutional channels — itself one of the internal mechanisms of polymorphic stability. System stability and the individual search for meaning run in parallel without canceling each other — answers to the question of meaning continue to be sought and built across paths, communities, and individuals.
