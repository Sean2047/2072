overview_en: |
  The equilibrium rests on three support layers: material (provisioning covers the subsistence floor), structural (collective action reduced in dimension — the electoral channel can resolve only distribution-layer questions), and experiential (collective memory of conflict fatigue). Three tensions persist: asymmetric disregard, dependence on provisioning's continued operation, and inapplicability at the interstate level. Its holding also requires the subjective-side condition that V7 stay low, supported jointly by K-12 formation, cognitive defusing, and Polarization Fatigue.
