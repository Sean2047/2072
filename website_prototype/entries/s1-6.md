overview: |
  五条来源独立运作并可叠加：过渡期身份真空（主要来源，失去描述"我是谁"的语言的人极易被政治激活）；约二十年的裸露期（压力最集中）；代际断裂（父辈的应对方式不能自动传递）；半接入状态的痛苦（依赖够深而红利不够）；地缘冲突与意识形态动员（改变形态而非方向）。动荡是过渡期的压力积累，稳定是压力消化之后的形态固化。
title_en: Real Sources of Turbulence
summary_en: There is no single Turbulent Period, only the pressure accumulation and digestion that each path undergoes at its own pace.
overview_en: |
  Five sources operate independently and can compound: the transitional Identity Vacuum (the principal source — people who lose the language to describe who they are are highly susceptible to political activation); the roughly twenty-year Exposure Window (where pressure is most concentrated); generational rupture (parents' ways of coping do not transmit automatically); the pain of the Semi-Connected State (dependence deep enough while the dividends fall short); geopolitical conflict and ideological mobilization (which change the form, not the direction). Turbulence is the accumulation of transitional pressure; stability is the form that hardens once the pressure is digested.
