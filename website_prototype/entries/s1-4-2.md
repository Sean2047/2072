overview: |
  AI使流动的核心收益系统性缩水，配给覆盖生存后离开不再必要，本地社会资本在流动中全部归零——流动净收益从正转负。人口留下的正反馈使地方分化固化，但存在关键不对称：人口跌破维持人际服务经济的临界密度时反馈逆转，学校关闭往往是触发点，进入逆向链条后几乎无法逆转——这是A1低产出社区轨迹分化的根本原因。
title_en: Mechanism of Migration Decline (Economically Driven)
summary_en: Net returns to migration turn from positive to negative, making settlement the rational choice; the positive feedback is nonlinear in the direction of population loss.
overview_en: |
  AI systematically shrinks the core returns to migration; once provisioning covers subsistence, leaving is no longer necessary, and local social capital is entirely written off in a move — net returns to migration turn from positive to negative. The positive feedback of people staying hardens local divergence, but with a key asymmetry: when population falls below the critical density that sustains an interpersonal-services economy the feedback reverses, school closure is often the trigger, and once the reverse chain begins it is almost impossible to undo — the root cause of diverging trajectories among A1's low-output communities.
